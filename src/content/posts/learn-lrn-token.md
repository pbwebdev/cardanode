We're starting to distribute our Learn Token which is our rewards for our learning management platform.

We'll be educating users about Cardano, projects on Cardano and other blockchain related products and services.

Users will be asked to complete an online quiz about what they have learnt from the online course and will be rewarded in LRN.

The LRN tokens will be exchangeable to ADA at certain buy backs and hopefully on a decentralised exchange or swap.

We're hoping to grow and education the community around Cardano.

Sign up to our mailing list to find out more as we progress with development.
