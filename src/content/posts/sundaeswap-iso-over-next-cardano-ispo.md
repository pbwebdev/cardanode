Find out more about the [AADA Finance ISPO](https://aada.gitbook.io/aada/ispo/ispo-participate). There ISPO will continue until they finish distributing their tokens.

## Ardana Stake Pool Alliance ISPO

Ardana are offering $DANA tokens for users that are staking at the various stake pools that are a part of the Ardana Stake Pool Alliance, which we ADAOZ is a part of.

The team at Ardana have witnessed large amounts of ADA consolidate around a few stake pools in the community. The market desires these tokens and it is clear to see with the delegation that has moved for them. With over 50 pools in the alliance, a delegate to choose any of these pools to be able to earn more $DANA tokens.

Furthermore, there are incentives for users to stake for a longer period of time to be able to earn bonus tokens. That creates staking loyalty.

Check out all the [stake pools that are a part of the alliance](https://docs.ardana.org/faq/ardana-stake-pool-alliance-aspa).

## Maladex

Maladex is a research-driven decentralised exchange designed and built by traditional finance veterans, quants, and Haskell natives. Their ISPO is being used to raise funds for the development of their platform and distribute their token, MAL which will be used on their DEX and as a governance token.

Their pools are 99% fee pools which means you will negate your ADA rewards for MAL tokens.

Learn more about [Maladex](https://maladex.com/) and their [stake pools for the ISPO](https://maladex.com/pools).

Ends at epoch 327

## anetaBTC LISO

Aneta provides the ability to have Bitcoin on Cardano and Ergo by wrapping it and creating a synthetic version of it. The original BTC is locked and the new one is minted on the new chain. This allows Bitcoin holders to transfer their value from one chain to another directly.

The anetaBTC ISPO is called a Liquidity Initial Stake Offering (LISO) with the aim to create as much liquidity and distribution of the cNETA token as possible. You can delegate to one of the two stake pools NETA1 or NETA2 to earn cNETA.

Learn more about the [LISO](https://medium.com/@anetaBTC/anetabtcs-liquidity-iso-d9ead13dd0eb) and more about [LISO Pools](https://www.anetabtc.io/liso-faq).
