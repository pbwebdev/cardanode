In preparation for the Cardano [Mary Hard Fork](https://iohk.io/en/blog/posts/2021/02/18/building-native-tokens-on-cardano-for-pleasure-and-profit/) on March 1st, we've done a few stake pool upgrades, and tweaks to the servers to ensure we're ready.

## Stake Pool Upgrades

-   A few weeks ago, we had already performed our stake pool upgrades to the latest version of the Cardano Node, version 1.25.1.
-   Our server environment was update and security measures all checked.
-   Pool keys check
-   CNTools upgrade to 8.0.0
-   Relay and producer node configurations checked
-   Relay and producer resources checked

At this point, our pool has yet to produce a block. We'll see what happens with the stake pool upgrades. It's a bit of a waiting game at the moment but we are sure with the support that we have our time will come.

In the meantime, we'll keep on tweaking, improving, testing and updating our delegators and Cardano community on our efforts.
