From time to time you may need to be able to access the unique stake key of your wallet.

Cardano wallets have this unique stake key to identify a wallet to a particular Cardano stake pool.

## How to Get Your Cardano Stake Address on Yoroi Wallet

### Load your Wallet

Load your wallet from the chrome extension.

### Navigate to Receive

From the top menu across the top of the wallet dashboard, click on the top tab menu "Receive"

### Navigate to the Rewards Data

From the left side menu click "Rewards"

### Obtain the Stake Address

This will load the Rewards wallet data and your Stake address will appear on the dashboard. Click the Copy icon to copy the stake address to be able to share it elsewhere.
