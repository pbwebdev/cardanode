### KYC Process

All exchanges have a Know Your Customer (KYC) process where you need to verify your identity with the exchange. This is to comply with various regulatory laws and anti-money laundering laws. You will need to upload identification in the form of an identification card, passport and utility bill with your address on it.

### Transferring from Crypto.com to Your Daedalus or Yoroi wallet

You won't be able to delegate and stake your ADA from the Crypto exchange but you can easily transfer it to your own wallet.

Click on your ADA balance in the app, and click TRANSFER and then WITHDRAW. This will start the process where you can designate the receiving address in an EXTERNAL WALLET.

If this is your first time transferring from an exchange, it is a good idea to do a test transfer with a small amount of ADA to ensure you have got the process correct. Please note that there is a 2 ADA transfer fee from the exchange to a wallet.
